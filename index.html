About

I am a B.Sc. Industrial Engineering & Management student (Data Science specialization) at Braude College, currently shipping audit desktop tools and full-stack demos alongside coursework.

My background is in process improvement, supply-chain thinking, optimization, and data analysis — from manufacturing floor work and corporate planning to migrating audit tooling from VBA to Python.

I build desktop apps, web services, and automation that solve real operational problems. AI tools (Cursor agents, RAG, Gemini APIs) help me implement faster; industrial engineering training drives what to measure, what to optimize, and what is worth shipping.

How I work

  • Problem framing — Define the process, constraints, and metrics before writing code (IE habit: measure, then improve).
  • Implementation — Python, full-stack web (React, Node, Flask), desktop shells (PyWebView), APIs, and version-controlled releases.
  • AI tooling — Cursor and local RAG for grounded development; Gemini and similar APIs where products need generated summaries or monitoring — always with clear human review.
